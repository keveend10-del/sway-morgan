---

## 1. Purpose

Sway is a participation platform that enables venues to understand who visits, who returns, and what participation patterns look like over time. This Agreement governs a paid pilot deployment of Sway at the Venue for the purpose of validating the participation system in a real operating environment.

---

## 2. What Sway Does

During the pilot:

- Sway places QR code check-in materials at designated locations on the Venue premises
- Guests opt in voluntarily by scanning and creating a Sway profile
- Each scan creates a participation record (place, timestamp, return history)
- The Venue receives access to a dashboard showing participation data (unique visitors, return rates, session patterns)
- No POS replacement, tab management, or payment processing occurs
- No alcohol-consumption rewards or incentive mechanics are deployed
- No changes to existing staff workflow are required

---

## 3. Venue Permission

The Venue grants Sway permission to:

- Place QR code materials (table cards, stickers, poster signage) on Venue premises at agreed locations
- Operate the Sway check-in system during Venue operating hours
- Collect voluntary opt-in participation data from Venue guests who choose to use Sway

The Venue may request removal or relocation of any Sway materials at any time with 48-hour notice.

---

## 4. Dashboard Access

Sway will provide the Venue with access to a web dashboard showing:

- Unique visitor count
- Return rate (guests with 2+ visits)
- Session frequency distribution
- Participation trends over the pilot period

Data is venue-specific. Sway does not share one venue's identifiable data with another venue.

---

## 5. Pilot Term

The pilot runs for **60–90 days**, beginning on the Effective Date. The exact end date is agreed upon at signing and noted here: **[End Date]**.

Either party may end the pilot early with **7 days written notice** (email is sufficient).

---

## 6. Pilot Fee

The Venue pays Sway **$99/month** for the duration of the pilot period.

Payment is due at the start of each month. Invoiced by Sway to **[Billing Contact / Email]**.

If either party ends the pilot early, the Venue is not charged for the remaining unused days of any prepaid month.

---

## 7. User Data and Privacy

- Guests participate voluntarily. No guest is required to use Sway as a condition of venue entry or service.
- Sway collects: name, email, venue check-in timestamp, and return visit data.
- Guest data is not sold to third parties.
- Guest data is not shared with the Venue in personally identifiable form beyond what the Venue dashboard displays in aggregate or individual profile format per platform design.
- Sway may use anonymized, aggregated metrics from this pilot (e.g., "X% return rate across pilot venues") in investor materials, pitch decks, and press — without identifying the Venue by name unless the Venue grants written permission.

---

## 8. No Staff Workflow Change Required

Sway does not require Venue staff to explain, promote, or operate any part of the Sway system. Staff participation is voluntary. Any staff who choose to assist guests in scanning do so at their own discretion.

---

## 9. No Exclusivity

This Agreement does not grant Sway exclusivity at the Venue and does not prevent the Venue from using other check-in, loyalty, analytics, or hospitality platforms during the pilot period.

---

## 10. Intellectual Property

Sway retains all intellectual property rights to the Sway platform, software, dashboard, and data infrastructure. Nothing in this Agreement transfers any IP rights to the Venue.

The Venue retains all rights to its own operational data, staff, and business processes. Nothing in this Agreement grants Sway any claim over Venue operations or proprietary business information.

---

## 11. Limitation of Liability

Sway is provided on an as-is basis during this pilot. Sway makes no guarantees about specific business outcomes, revenue lift, or return rate improvement. The pilot exists to observe whether the participation system produces useful signal — not to guarantee a specific result.

Neither party is liable to the other for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages arising from this Agreement.

---

## 12. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with **7 days written notice** sent to the contact emails below. Upon termination:

- Sway removes all QR code materials from the Venue within 5 business days
- Dashboard access ends at the close of the notice period
- Any prepaid pilot fees for days not yet used are refunded pro-rata

---

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.

---

## 14. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the full understanding between Sway and the Venue regarding this pilot. Changes require written consent from both parties.
